# Research Methods Lab

## Welcome

This platform explores research design, logic of inquiry, and evidence-based reasoning.

Research is not a technique. It is a structured way of thinking.

---

## Structure of the Lab

### Module 1 — Ontology & Epistemology
What is knowledge? What counts as evidence?

### Module 2 — Research Design Logic
How do we move from questions to structure?

### Module 3 — Data & Measurement
How do we operationalize concepts?

### Module 4 — Causality & Inference
What allows us to claim relationships?

### Module 5 — Methods Toolbox
Qualitative, quantitative, mixed, and computational approaches.

---

## Course Philosophy

Research methods are not mechanical procedures.  
They are structured decision systems under uncertainty.
